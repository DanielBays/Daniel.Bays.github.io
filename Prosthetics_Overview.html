About us

As the nation’s leading prosthetic care provider, we help thousands of people with limb loss or limb difference achieve mobility and independence each year. Our clinicians receive ongoing training and education in the most current innovations, and our broad, collaborative expertise translates into the highest quality of care for each person we serve.

Lower Limb

We design custom prosthetic solutions for all levels of lower limb loss using clinically-appropriate technology and state-of-the-art components.
