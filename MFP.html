MyFitnessPal Evaluation

For this project, my team and I conducted a heuristic evaution of the popluation fitness app called MyFitnessPal. Each member went through each page and noted any poor design or usability problems we came across. Once every member completed their evaluations of the app, we came back together and went through each usability problem, discussing the issue the finalizing a severity score. I created a wireframe that I believe would help users feel more connected to app.
